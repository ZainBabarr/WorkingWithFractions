Fractions Lesson 1: Rules and Basics



Fractions are a way to represent parts of a whole number.

They consist of a numerator (the number on top) and a denominator (the number on the bottom), separated by a fraction bar.

Fractions can be added, subtracted, multiplied, and divided, and can be represented in different forms such as proper fractions, improper fractions, and mixed numbers.





Fractions can also be represented in many ways such as pie graphs, decimals, and percentages
